About Steam Web Scraping CRM

Steam market operations, organized from source setup to monitoring.

Steam Web Scraping CRM helps teams configure Steam market sources, manage catalog metadata, run scraping workflows, and monitor priority market targets from one focused workspace.

Built for repeatable market intelligence

The platform connects catalog setup, scraping modes, wish and watch tracking, exports, and background checks so operators can move faster with cleaner data.

Mission

Reduce the manual work behind Steam market monitoring.

The CRM exists to turn scattered Steam market checks into a repeatable operating flow. Instead of rebuilding context every time, teams can define sources, enrich them with catalog data, run the right scraping workflow, and keep high-value targets visible over time.
